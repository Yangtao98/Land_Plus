Getting you ready

Introduction & Planning

We understand a move is more than simply changing locations. It's often a monumental change to people's lives as well. That's why our experts are available 24/7 to not only make sure things go to plan but to also assure that you're never alone in the process.





Home-Finding

Our main objective is to find you suitable housing that fits your requirement and secure it through optimal negotiation. Our realtor and consultant will ensure you are guided with all documentation and market practise, check in support with property condition report and attending to defects in the first month.




Settling In

This a post move settling in program with the aim of accelerating the familiarization process. Program includes topics that may cover conversion of driving license, domestic help, car leasing or purchase, leisure options, or recreational club.




Immigration Assistance

We have the expertise to direct you strategically on the application process, document and data requirements and estimated processing times. We prepare and file applications, track visa status and renewals, helping assignees, families and clients remain compliant with MOM guideline.




School Search

We provide information of schools offering a specific country national or international system. Our consultant will fix appointment with schools' administrators and accompany you to schools and brief you on the process of enrolment and administrative guidance.




Temporary Accomodation

We worked with leading serviced apartments and corporate housings to provide you temporary accommodation that offer the conveniences of a hotel but the comfort of a real home. This is a transition home for you while you get your new permanent home.

Property Management

This support is extended to transferee who has been relocated abroad and need ongoing support for maintenance of his residential property in Singapore. Some of the service includes property condition inspection, as intermediary for your Tenant and lease negotiations.




Car Hire, Leasing & Purchase

Owning a car costs serious money in Singapore. Therefore, car rental and leasing could be attractive alternatives especially for short term assignments. We are in partnership with reputable car leasing companies that will do cost comparative analysis to recommend if car leasing or purchase makes better financial sense for you.




Area Orientation

This tour is intended to provide sufficient local area knowledge during pre-view or early onset of an assignment. The program covers matters related to banking, expat neighbourhoods, local customs, medical facilities, accessibility to daily necessities.




Tenancy Management

An ongoing support either for a periodical term or throughout the tenancy. Support includes sourcing and recommendation of contractors for works required. We provide guidance on maintenance work and cost assessment. Negotiation for dilapidation when dispute on cost and liability arises.




Airport Meet & Greet

First impression lasts and our friendly drivers readily welcomes you and your family after a long day of travel and brings you safely to your desired location. We ensure our multi purpose vehicles are regularly checked for safety and cleanliness.
